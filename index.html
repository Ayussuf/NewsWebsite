A Somali Imam is Using Storytelling To Change Society and Political System

Stories matter. People make sense of the world through stories and shape up how we understand it. Somalia requires new stories, but people will listen when they themselves are included in the story-line. An Imam at a mosque in Somalia’s capital, Mogadishu, is doing exactly that, offering a new narrative to show what is possible. Sheikh Abdi Hayi is shifting gears and is bringing new ways of telling stories in an unlikely place: a mosque.

The City Of Hargeisa

The primitive caves of Laas Geel are situated near the city of Hargeisa. This city is the most prominent in Somaliland. Though it suffered a lot during the civil war still, at present, it is recovering slowly. The most interesting attraction of this city is the Hargeisa Provincial Museum. This museum was founded in 1972. Since then it is depicting the cultural as well as the ethnological history of Somaliland. Currently, this museum has a collection of 1400 cultural and art objects. A must visit place for a visitor who likes such attraction.

The Cave Art of Laas Geel

This is perhaps the most famous attraction in the country. It is situated in the vicinity of Hargeisa, Somalia. Here a series of caves have spectacular rock paintings showing animals, people, etc by primitive inhabitants of this land. Historians say that these amazing paintings are artworks from 9000BC. Tourists who are fond of seeing primitive art and culture should not miss this place in Somalia.

In Sweden, more Somalis are dying of Coronavirus than any other group

A local doctor suggested the virus may have spread during Friday prayers at a local mosque, but blamed authorities for not spreading information about how the disease is transmitted earlier.

Sea Beaches of Berbera

While you are in Hargeisa do not miss the chance to visit the virgin beaches in Berbera of Somalia. These beaches have golden sands with mesmerising blue-green sea. If you have got a bit bored seeing historic places and art objects then the beaches of Berbera will certainly provide you the relaxation you need. The sea of this region in Somalia has some great coral reefs too where a tourist can enjoy snorkeling or scuba diving.
